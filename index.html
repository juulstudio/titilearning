Gamified learning experience with Titi Learning

We are developing a fun but educational game about sexual health for young refugee girls (9-16 years old) in Sub-Saharan Africa to better prevent teenage pregnancy! Our game collects data about the girls and their knowledge level within various topics such as pregnancy, STDs, period and other sexual and reproductive health related concerns.
